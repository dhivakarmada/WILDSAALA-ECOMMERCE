Sustainability

Conscious streetwear. Made to endure.

Organic Heavyweight Cotton

Our hoodies and tees are constructed from 100% organic cotton, sourced from ethical suppliers that minimize water usage and eliminate chemical pesticides.

Circular Recycling Program

We design garments for longevity, but when your armor is fully spent, we accept used Wild Saala products back to shred and recycle into new yarns.

Zero-Plastic Packaging

Every order is wrapped in recycled acid-free tissue paper and shipped in 100% compostable bio-pouches made of cornstarch and PBAT.

Slow Fashion Manifesto

Fast fashion is a digital virus. We produce in small batches to match demand precisely. We do not mark down products or trigger artificial overproduction. Buy fewer, buy heavier, and wear forever.
