Portal FAQs // DECRYPTERS

Hacking guidelines and answers for future co-creators.

How do I get paid for a design collaboration?
We credit a standard 7% revenue share on all sales generated by approved drops containing your signature design. Payouts are dispatched monthly directly to your local Indian bank account via UPI or IMPS.
Who owns the Intellectual Property rights of designs?
You maintain the copyright of your original designs. By submitting and launching a drop, you grant Wild Saala a license to manufacture and sell the print. Rights agreements are signed before drop release.
What file formats are accepted for design proposals?
For vector designs, we accept high-res PDF or SVG files. For illustrations or mockups, high-resolution PNG or TIFF files (300 DPI minimum) work best. Videos should be MP4 (H.264).
