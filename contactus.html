FAQs: THE WILD & THE CLEAR

Got questions? We've got answers. If your question isn't here, hit us up on the form above.

What is "WILD SAALA"?

WILD SAALA isn't just a clothing brand. It's a statement, a lifestyle. We're a luxury streetwear brand for the rebels, the creators, and the unapologetically wild souls of India. We don't follow trends; we create culture.

What's this 400 GSM buzz?

400 GSM stands for 400 grams per square meter - it's how we measure the thickness and quality of our hoodies. Most brands use 250-300 GSM fabric, but we went extra thicc for that premium feel and warmth. It's like wearing a hug from the streets.

Where's my damn hoodie? (Shipping)

We ship within 2-3 business days after your order is placed. Delivery usually takes 3-5 days within India. You'll get a tracking link via SMS/Email once your hoodie ships. If it's taking longer than expected, hit us up at support@wildsaala.com and we'll chase it down for you.

Do you ship to the jungle (NE states)?

Hell yeah we do! We ship across all Indian states including the beautiful Northeast. Shipping might take 1-2 days longer to those areas, but your hoodie will find its way to you. If you're in a particularly remote area, drop us a message before ordering and we'll confirm.

How do I pick the right size?

Our hoodies run true to size but with an oversized fit. If you like a regular fit, go with your normal size. If you want that proper oversized streetwear look, size up once. Check our size guide for exact measurements. When in doubt, DM us on Instagram - we'll help you out.

What's your Return Policy?

With proper evidence and reason, we will take a return. However, please note that the money will not be refunded. Instead, you will receive a token amount or brand loyalty points equivalent to the purchase value, which you can use for your next order. The return must be initiated within 3 days of delivery.

What's your Exchange Policy?

If the reason for the exchange is our mistake (e.g., wrong size, color, or a manufacturing defect), we'll happily take care of the exchange and cover all delivery charges. If you're exchanging for any other reason, you'll be required to bear the delivery charges.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) with a ₹20 handling fee, UPI payments, and Bank Transfers. Please note that credit/debit card, wallet, and "pay later" options are currently under maintenance.

Are your hoodies unisex?

Absolutely! All our hoodies are designed to be gender-neutral with a focus on oversized, comfortable fits that look great on everyone. We believe style has no gender, just attitude.

How do I wash my WILD SAALA hoodie?

To keep your hoodie wild, we recommend washing it inside-out in cold water on a gentle cycle. Air-drying is best, but if you must, tumble dry on low. Never use bleach or harsh detergents.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we do! Orders above a certain value are eligible for free shipping. Keep an eye out for our promotions and exclusive free shipping coupons that we share on our social media channels.

What if I receive a damaged product?

First off, our bad! We conduct a rigorous quality check, but if something slips through, please email us a clear photo of the damage within 24 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a free exchange immediately.

Are your products sustainable?

We're committed to making a difference. We use eco-friendly inks, carbon-neutral packaging, and strive to source sustainable materials wherever possible. It's a journey, but we're walking it.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can only be cancelled within 24 hours of being placed. Once the order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled. You can contact our support team to check the status of your order.

Do you have a physical store?

For now, we're an online-only brand. This allows us to cut costs and focus on providing you with the best quality products at the best prices. Follow us on social media for updates if we ever decide to go offline!

Can I collaborate with WILD SAALA?

If you're a content creator, artist, or a wild soul with a unique vision, we'd love to hear from you. Drop us a message on our contact form with a link to your work, and we'll get back to you.

What's the meaning behind the name?

It's an expression. A feeling. It's the rebel inside us all. It’s about being bold, authentic, and unapologetically yourself. It's a celebration of the raw, untamed spirit.

Why hoodies only?

We believe in doing one thing exceptionally well. For us, that's the hoodie. We've obsessed over the fit, the fabric, the feel, and the statement it makes. It’s our canvas to unleash our creativity.

How do your limited drops work?

Our limited drops are exclusive, small-batch collections that are released for a short time. Once they're sold out, they're gone for good. Sign up for our newsletter to get notified before anyone else!

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is shipped, we'll send you an email and SMS with a tracking number and a link to track your package's journey right to your doorstep. You can also log in to your account to view your order status.

Do you offer gift cards?

Not yet, but we're working on it! A WILD SAALA gift card is coming soon. Stay tuned to our social media and website for updates on when you can unleash the gift of wild style.

How can I join the "Wild Tribe" community?

You're already in! Follow us on Instagram, subscribe to our newsletter, and tag your posts with #WILDSAALA. We regularly feature our tribe members and hold exclusive contests and events just for you.

Is my personal information secure?

Your privacy is our priority. We use secure encryption and payment gateways to ensure all your personal and payment information is protected. We will never share or sell your data.

What if I have an issue with an order?

The best way to get help is to fill out the contact form above, giving us your order number and a clear description of the issue. Our support team will get back to you within 24 hours.

Do you have a size guide?

Yes, our comprehensive size guide is available in the footer of our website. It provides detailed measurements for each style to help you find your perfect fit.
