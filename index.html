WILD SAALA CARE GUIDE

Don't just wear it, live in it. Here’s how to extend the life of your hoodie and keep it wild.

👕 Wash Wild, Not Reckless.

Your hoodie is a living statement. Treat it with respect. Following these steps will ensure its color and fit stay true, just like your style.

  • Wash inside-out to protect the print.
  • Use cold water and a gentle cycle.
  • Don't use bleach or harsh detergents.
  • Air dry it or tumble dry on low heat.
🧼 Stain Slay.

Life happens, stains happen. Here’s how to fight back without damaging the fabric.

  • Coffee/Tea: Blot the area, then dab with a mix of water and vinegar.
  • Grease/Oil: Sprinkle cornstarch or baby powder, let it absorb, then wash.
  • Mud: Let it dry completely, then brush off. Dab with a mild detergent solution.
  • Always test a small, hidden area first!
🩹 Mend & Conquer.

A tear is not the end. It's an opportunity for a new statement. Embrace visible mending and make your hoodie a one-of-a-kind piece of art.

  • Patch-working: Add a patch from an old t-shirt or another garment to cover a hole.
  • Hand-stitching: Use a contrasting thread color to sew up a tear, making the repair a deliberate design element.
  • Embroidery: Cover a small stain or tear with a unique, hand-stitched design.
♻️ Upcycle & Unleash.

When your hoodie has seen its last battle, don't throw it away. Transform it into something new and useful.

  • Tote Bag: Cut off the sleeves and hood, stitch the bottom, and use the sleeves for handles.
  • Cushion Cover: Stuff the body with old fabric and stitch it closed for a custom cushion.
  • Crop Top: Simply cut the bottom for a fresh new look.

FAQs: WILD & CLEAR

Got questions? We've got answers

Where's my damn hoodie? (Shipping)

We ship within 2-3 business days after your order is placed. Delivery usually takes 3-5 days within India. You'll get a tracking link via SMS/Email once your hoodie ships. If it's taking longer than expected, hit us up at support@wildsaala.com and we'll chase it down for you.

What's this 400 GSM buzz?

400 GSM stands for 400 grams per square meter - it's how we measure the thickness and quality of our hoodies. Most brands use 250-300 GSM fabric, but we went extra thicc for that premium feel and warmth. It's like wearing a hug from the streets.

Do you ship to the jungle (NE states)?

Hell yeah we do! We ship across all Indian states including the beautiful Northeast. Shipping might take 1-2 days longer to those areas, but your hoodie will find its way to you. If you're in a particularly remote area, drop us a message before ordering and we'll confirm.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy if the hoodie doesn't fit or isn't what you expected. It must be unworn, unwashed, and with all tags attached. We don't cover return shipping unless it's our mistake. For exchanges, we'll cover the shipping back to you. Hit up support@wildsaala.com to initiate a return.

How do I pick the right size?

Our hoodies run true to size but with an oversized fit. If you like a regular fit, go with your normal size. If you want that proper oversized streetwear look, size up once. Check our size chart (link in footer) for exact measurements. When in doubt, DM us on Instagram - we'll help you out.
