How to Measure

  • Chest: Place the measuring tape around the fullest part of your chest, right under your armpits.
  • Shoulder: Measure from the edge of one shoulder to the other, straight across your back.
  • Sleeve Length: Measure from the top of your shoulder down to your wrist.
  • Total Length: Measure from the highest point on your shoulder down to the bottom hem of the garment.

Pro Tips:

  • For an oversized fit, we recommend sizing up one size from your usual.
  • If you're between sizes, consider your preferred fit. Size down for a more snug fit, or size up for a looser, more relaxed feel.
  • Compare with your favorite hoodie. Lay a hoodie you already love flat and measure it, then compare those measurements to our chart.
