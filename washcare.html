The Care Code

Rules for rebels. A guide to preserving your threads and staying wild.

The Wash Ritual

Your hoodie is a statement. Here's how to treat it like one and ensure it stays true to its core.

Wash in Cold Water

To protect the fabric and maintain vibrant colors, always wash your garment inside out in cold water on a gentle cycle.

Air Dry in Shade

Hang your hoodie to dry in the shade. High heat from a tumble dryer can cause shrinkage and damage the print.

Do Not Wring

Gently squeeze out excess water. Wringing can stretch the fibers and distort the shape of the garment.

No Ironing on Print

The graphics are a key part of your wild style. Iron your hoodie inside out and avoid direct contact with the print.

Do Not Dry Clean

The chemicals used in dry cleaning can be harsh on our fabrics and prints. Hand wash or machine wash as instructed.

Storage Secrets

Fold your hoodie rather than hanging it to prevent stretching. Store it in a cool, dry place to protect the fabric.

Embrace the Imperfect: Mend It Wildly

A tear is not a mistake; it's a new chapter. Turn a flaw into a feature with these rebel tips.

  • A tear is a story. Use a contrasting thread to sew up rips and make the repair a deliberate design element.
  • Stains can be covered with a custom embroidered patch, turning a flaw into a unique feature.
  • When your hoodie is ready for a new life, upcycle it into a tote bag, cushion cover, or anything else you can imagine.
